Claude's Favourite Cookies

(previously the Claude Baked Good Redistribution Service)

My speciality is cookies, but I also make other things!

I used to develop recipes - but our oven is currently broken :(

In place of this, here is a collection of my favourite recipes!

Treat of the month


It was of course my birthday in June - now solidly in my late twenties! I had a broad range of Singaporean desserts, some I liked (mango pudding) more than others (chendol). There is always the classic dish of Kaya toast which is plain white toast with a thick layer of a sweet but not cloying coconut spread.

Treats I'm looking forward to!

  • I'm serving this cake at my birthday party later in July. ENZE cakes are nice, light and fluffy and not too sweet. I like the bitter notes of hojicha, so I have high hopes for this!
  • I'm on the hunt for a crispy pain au chocolat - maybe it's time to revisit a local bakery?
  • Also I'm going to recipe develop some sort of no bake recipe - probably some sort of modified pie or cheesecake with a Kaya flavour? I've had enough tiramisu for a bit...
